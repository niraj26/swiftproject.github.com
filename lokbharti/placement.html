Placement

There is no formal cell but our principal and Lecturers are providing necessary guidance regularly. The successful trained Student's join services into the following categories of respective institutes

  • As Teachers in Govt. Primary Schools, Secondary Schools and Primary Teacher's Training College and as Education Inspector
  •  
  • As Teachers in grant-in-aid secondary, Higher Secondary and PTC Colleges approved by the Government .
  •  
  • Students also have batter opportunities in NGO's.

Most of the trainees are appointed as the teachers in all over Gujarat
