Welcome to GBTC

GBTC ( Graduate Basic Training College ), Lokbharti - Sanosara was established in June-1969.

GBTC, Lokbharti runs Teacher Education Course keeping in view The Gandhian Philosophy and Nai Talim.

It aims at preparing trained knowledgeable Primary School Supervisors, Administrative officers, Teachers for PTC Colleges, Secondary and Higher Secondary Schools.

Offered Courses

  • Training in Community Life
  • Co-Education
  • Social Work and Rural Activities
  • Educational Tours
  • Visits to unique organization
