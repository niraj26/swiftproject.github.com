Air Chiller:

We are manufacturing a wide range of air chiller that is widely suitable for its high operational efficiency. Our air chillers are designed to cool both the process as well as the process machine. Using an air chiller to remove heat from the process involves an evaporator to remove the heat and an air cooled condenser to remove the heat from the evaporator. In a typical plant layout, the evaporator and condenser are inside the chiller unit.

Salient Features :

  • High quality components and robust design.
  • Factory built and tested for quality check.
  • User friendly micro-processor based control panel.
  • Chillers are fully automatic with high quality controls.
  • Quick chilling is ensured with plate type and shell and tube evaporators.
